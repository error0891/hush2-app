Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Hush ("the App") is a sound therapy application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Hush works entirely on your device. No account is required, no email is collected, and no usage data is transmitted to our servers.

Audio Processing

All sounds are generated locally on your device using the Web Audio API. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Subscriptions

If you subscribe to Hush Pro, the purchase is processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription management (including cancellation) is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Feedback Form

If you voluntarily submit feedback through the in-app form, your responses are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data.

Analytics & Tracking

Hush does not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, cookies, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Hush does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hushapp@proton.me.
